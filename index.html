Global Air Pollution Graph

Air pollution has become an issue of global concern, affecting both our environment and the health of the world's population. The alteration of the atmosphere by harmful chemicals has been linked to various respiratory and cardiovascular conditions. This dataset aims to provide insights into air quality metrics across various cities and countries.

Figure 1.2 ArcChart

Countries span three distinct AQI categories, from the cleanest (Good) to more polluted (Unhealthy). Most European countries, including France, Netherlands, and Poland, are classified under the 'Good' AQI category. India and Pakistan's classification under 'Unhealthy' is alarming, underscoring the significant air quality challenges in the South Asian region, often attributed to factors like industrialization, vehicular emissions, and crop burning.
